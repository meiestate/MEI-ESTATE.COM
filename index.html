What makes this different

✅ Admin Approved Listings

How it works (MVP)

  1. Broker or seller adds a property.
  2. Admin reviews and approves it.
  3. Approved listing appears publicly.
  4. Buyer or seller submits enquiry from homepage or dedicated pages.
  5. Lead shows up in dashboard CRM for follow-up.
